Kakinada Kaja

Kakinada Kaja> - Making a good Kakinada Kaja is very simple and this recipe is perfect for a home preparation. As the name says,it is a sweet delicacy from the place called Kakinada in Andhra pradesh.The word "khaja" originated from Arabic meaning sacred or pure.Kakinada kaja is just a replica of Ranchi khaja. kaja a puffy pastry strip soaked in sugar syrup literally melts in mouth.





Ingredients For Kakinada Kaja
  • 1.5 cups - All purpose floue
  • 2 tbsp- Gram flour
  • 1/8 tsp- Baking soda
  • 2 tbsp - Butter
  • Oil for Deep frying
  • 1.5 cups - Sugar
  • 2.5 cups - Water
  • 1 tsp - Cardamom powder
